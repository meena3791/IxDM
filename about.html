Subhashini Ganapathy

Lab Director

Subhashini Ganapathy, Ph.D.

Associate Professor, Biomedical, Industrial & Human Factors Dept

Surgery Faculty Program Director of Sensor Systems Research

Wright State University, Dayton, OH 45435

(937) 775-5044; subhashini.ganapathy@wright.edu

Dr. Ganapathy is an Associate Professor in the Department of Biomedical, Industrial, and Human Factors Engineering. She possesses a comprehensive, in-depth knowledge of human interaction, experimental design, and user-experience testing methodologies. She has worked on defining human behavior and user interaction for future technology. She also has more than 12 years of research experience in model-based aiding, human behavioral modeling, and adaptive user interface design.

Before joining WSU, Dr. Ganapathy worked as a Senior User Experience Researcher at Intel Corporation. She has worked on defining human behavior and user interaction for future technologies such as investigating how different multimodal interaction techniques support effective interaction and learning in consumer products, and how novel interaction modalities can enhance the traditional experience. She is an IEEE senior member, SWE member, member of the National Society of Professional Engineers (NSPE) as an EI and INFORMS member. She also serves on the technical committee for Human Computer Interaction, IEEE SMC Society.
